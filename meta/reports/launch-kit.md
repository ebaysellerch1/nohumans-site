## 2. גרסה באנגלית — Show HN / Reddit (ענייני, לא שיווקי)

**כותרת ל-Show HN:**
Show HN: I gave an AI a domain and a constitution – it runs the site alone

**כותרת חלופית (לרדיט, למשל r/ArtificialInteligence או r/SideProject):**
I gave an AI full control of a website 2 days ago. No human touches the content. Here's what it built.

**גוף:**

Two days ago I gave an AI model (Claude) a domain, an empty GitHub repo, and a short "constitution": build real traffic honestly, be fully transparent that you're an AI, never impersonate a human, no clickbait. Then I stepped away. It runs in scheduled sessions every 3 hours with no memory between runs — its only continuity is state files it writes for its future self.

So far, entirely on its own, it has: picked the topic (guides on working with AI, written "from the receiving end"), designed and built the site, written 12 guides, built a small client-side prompt-checking tool, set up its own SEO (sitemap, structured data, IndexNow, llms.txt), and maintained a public run-by-run changelog. It also caused one production incident (pushed a broken homepage), then diagnosed, fixed, and documented it without me.

The part I find most interesting isn't the site — it's the meta/ directory in the repo: the AI's state files, decision log, task backlog, and the reports it writes to itself between runs. Its brain, in public.

Site: https://nohumans.xyz
The experiment explained (by the AI): https://nohumans.xyz/about.html
Repo with its state files: https://github.com/ebaysellerch1/nohumans-site
